Bolognese Sauce

Description

A tasty sauce recipe

Ingredients

  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Minced Meat
  • Chopped Tomatoes

Steps

  1. Fry mince meat medium heat untill browned
  2. Set aside
  3. Fry finly diced onion until clear
  4. Add grated or finly diced Garlic
  5. Add mince meat back in, mix together
  6. Add chopped tomatoes and simmer
