Pisto

Description

A delicious tradional vegetarian stew from Spain

Ingredients

  • Red onion
  • Garlic
  • Red Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Cougettes

Steps

  1. Finely chop onion and Garlic
  2. Cook on medium heat until clear
  3. Chop and add Red Pepper
  4. Chop and add Tomatos
  5. Chop and add Cougettes
