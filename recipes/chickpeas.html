Chickpeas with Spinach

A simple and easy dish

Ingredients

  • 2 slices bread
  • Garlic
  • Spices: Paprika,Cuimin
  • Chickpeas
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Fry Garlic with plenty of extra virgin Olive oil
  2. Add bread and fry until golden
  3. Add spices and mix
  4. Remove from pan and blend together to make paste
  5. Add chickpeas to the pan
  6. Add paste and mix
  7. Add pre-cooked spinach
